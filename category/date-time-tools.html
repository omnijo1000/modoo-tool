Frequently Asked Questions
How does the Age Calculator handle different calendar systems?▼
The Age Calculator uses the Gregorian calendar and calculates exact years, months, and days. The Korean Age Calculator separately follows the Korean system where everyone gains a year on January 1st, regardless of their birthday.
What counts as a business day?▼
The Business Days Calculator excludes weekends (Saturday and Sunday). It does not automatically exclude public holidays, so add your country's specific holidays manually as needed.
What is the Pomodoro Technique?▼
The Pomodoro Technique is a time-management method: work for 25 minutes, take a 5-minute break, repeat 4 cycles, then take a longer break. Our Pomodoro Timer automates this cycle with audio notifications to keep you on track.
How do I convert a Unix timestamp to a readable date?▼
Use the Timestamp Converter — paste any Unix timestamp in seconds or milliseconds and it instantly shows the equivalent human-readable date and time in your local timezone and UTC.
