Q. Navy method vs BMI method — which is more accurate?
The Navy method (using actual body measurements) is generally more accurate than the BMI method, especially for muscular or athletic individuals. For best results, use a DEXA scan or hydrostatic weighing.

Q. What is a healthy body fat percentage?
For men: 10–20% is generally considered healthy. For women: 18–28%. Athletes typically have lower body fat (men 6–13%, women 14–20%). Very low body fat can also be unhealthy.
