BMI Categories (WHO Standard)

Underweight: BMI < 18.5 · Normal: 18.5–24.9 · Overweight: 25–29.9 · Obese Class I: 30–34.9 · Obese Class II: 35–39.9 · Obese Class III: ≥ 40. Note: Asian BMI standards use lower cutoffs (Overweight ≥ 23, Obese ≥ 27.5).

Q. What is BMR?
BMR (Basal Metabolic Rate) is the number of calories your body burns at rest to maintain basic functions like breathing and circulation. Calculated using the Mifflin-St Jeor equation.

Q. Is BMI accurate?
BMI is a useful screening tool but does not measure body fat directly. Athletes may have a high BMI due to muscle mass. For a complete health assessment, consult a healthcare provider.
