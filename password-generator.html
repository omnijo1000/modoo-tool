What Makes a Strong Password?

A strong password should be at least 12 characters long and include a mix of uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols. Avoid dictionary words, names, or predictable patterns.

Q. How long should my password be?
12 characters is the minimum for security. 16+ is recommended for high-value accounts. A 16-character random password with all character types would take millions of years to crack with brute force.

Q. Is it safe to use an online password generator?
This generator runs entirely in your browser using the Web Crypto API — no passwords are sent to any server, stored, or logged. You can verify this by turning off your internet and it will still work.
