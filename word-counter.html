Q. How long does it take to read 1,000 words?
The average adult reading speed is 200–250 words per minute. 1,000 words ≈ 4–5 minutes. Speaking speed is typically 130–150 words per minute, so 1,000 words ≈ 6–7 minutes to speak.

Q. How is reading time calculated?
This tool uses 200 words per minute (average adult silent reading). Speak time uses 150 words per minute (comfortable presentation pace).
