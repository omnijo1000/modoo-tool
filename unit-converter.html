Common Unit Conversions

1 kilometer = 0.621371 miles · 1 kg = 2.20462 lbs · 0°C = 32°F · 1 meter = 3.28084 feet · 1 inch = 2.54 cm

Q. How many cm in an inch?
1 inch = 2.54 centimeters exactly. 1 foot = 30.48 cm. 1 yard = 91.44 cm.

Q. How to convert Fahrenheit to Celsius?
Formula: °C = (°F − 32) × 5/9. Example: 98.6°F = 37°C (body temperature).
