How to Calculate Fuel Cost

Metric: Fuel (L) = Distance(km) × Efficiency(L/100km) ÷ 100. Cost = Fuel × Price per liter.
Imperial: Fuel (gal) = Distance(miles) ÷ MPG. Cost = Fuel × Price per gallon.

Q. How do I improve fuel efficiency?
Maintain steady speed (cruise control on highways), keep tires properly inflated (+1 MPG per 10 PSI), remove roof racks when not in use, avoid idling more than 1 minute.

Q. L/100km vs MPG — how to convert?
MPG = 235.21 ÷ L/100km. For example, 8 L/100km ≈ 29.4 MPG. 6 L/100km ≈ 39.2 MPG. The lower the L/100km, the better the efficiency.
