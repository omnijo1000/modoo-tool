How to Calculate Age

Age is calculated by subtracting the birth date from the current date. For example, a person born on June 4, 1995 would be 31 years old as of June 4, 2026. Partial years are counted in months and days.

Q. How old am I if born in 1990?
A person born in 1990 is 35–36 years old in 2026, depending on whether their birthday has passed yet this year. Use this calculator for the exact date.

Q. What day of the week was I born?
This calculator shows the day of the week for any date of birth. It also shows your Western zodiac sign and Chinese zodiac animal year.
