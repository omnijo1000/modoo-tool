Tipping Guide by Country

USA: 18–20% at restaurants, 15% minimum. Tipping is culturally expected and forms a significant part of server income. Bars: $1–2/drink.

Europe (UK/France/Germany): 10–15% if service charge not included. Many restaurants add a 12.5% service charge automatically — check the bill first.

Australia/Canada: 10–15% appreciated but not obligatory. Growing tip culture in cities.

Asia (Japan/Korea/China): Tipping is generally not customary and can sometimes be considered rude. No tip needed in most restaurants.

India: 10% is common in upscale restaurants. Check if service charge (usually 5–10%) is already added.
